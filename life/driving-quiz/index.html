What is Gaimen Kirikae (外国免許切替)?

外免切替（外国免許切替）是外國人把母國駕照換成日本駕照的官方程序。在日本，符合資格的駕照持有人可以透過各都道府縣的運轉免許試驗場進行知識確認（筆試）與技能確認（路考），免去從零學起的駕訓班流程，大幅節省時間與費用。

筆試題目以日本交通規則為基礎，採是非題型，題庫涵蓋道路標誌、交通號誌、優先權判斷、停車規則、酒駕罰則、安全駕駛常識等主題。對於來自香港、台灣、英國、加拿大、澳洲等已簽訂協議國家的駕駛人，路考可能免除；對於來自中國、美國、菲律賓、印度等需要完整流程的國家駕駛人，模擬測驗更是準備路考前不可或缺的一環。

Gaimen Kirikae (外国免許切替) is the official procedure for foreign nationals to convert their home-country driving licence into a Japanese driving licence. Eligible licence holders can take a knowledge confirmation test (written exam) and skill confirmation test (driving exam) at a prefectural Driving Licence Test Centre (運転免許試験場), bypassing the lengthy and expensive driving-school process.

The written exam is based on Japanese traffic regulations and uses true/false questions. The question bank covers road signs, traffic signals, right-of-way judgement, parking rules, drink-driving penalties, and general safe-driving knowledge. For drivers from countries with bilateral agreements (Hong Kong, Taiwan, UK, Canada, Australia, etc.), the practical driving test may be waived. For drivers from China, the US, the Philippines, India, and other countries requiring the full process, mock testing is essential preparation.

Gaimen Kirikae Mock Test FAQ

外免切替筆試要考幾題？合格分數是多少？

日本各都道府縣略有不同，但通常為 10 道是非題，合格標準為 7 題以上正確（70 分）。部分試驗場會加考道路標誌題（5 道），總分需達 70% 以上。

哪些國家可以免路考？

與日本簽訂協議的國家如台灣、香港、英國、德國、法國、加拿大、澳洲、紐西蘭、韓國等約 29 個國家／地區可免路考。中國、美國、印度、菲律賓、越南等需要完整路考。可至各都道府縣警察局或運轉免許試驗場官網確認最新名單。

不合格可以重考嗎？需要等多久？

可以。通常隔日即可預約重考，部分試驗場當日可直接重考。重考需再次繳交考試費（約 ¥2,550）。建議第一次不過後，先針對錯題類型加強練習，再預約下次。

模擬測驗能完全替代正式題庫嗎？

不能 100% 替代，但能涵蓋約 80-90% 的常見題型與陷阱題。建議搭配運轉免許試驗場提供的官方教材，或 IsshoHub 的 日本駕照天書 一起準備。

是非題的判斷重點是什麼？

日本交通規則的是非題經常在例外條件上設陷阱，例如「在任何情況下都⋯⋯」「必定⋯⋯」這類絕對化敘述通常為錯誤。讀題時注意「必須」「可以」「禁止」「原則上」等關鍵詞。

沒登入也可以做測驗嗎？

可以，訪客模式可以完整答完 20 題並查看結果。登入後可以解鎖完整 100 題題庫、自動記錄錯題、查看歷次成績。

路考準備需要多久？

視個人駕駛經驗而定。一般建議在通過筆試後，安排 2-3 次運轉免許試驗場的試走，了解日本特有的考試路線設計（如 クランク、S字、踏切 等）。日本車道狹窄、考官嚴格，建議至少預留 1-2 個月準備。

How many questions are on the Gaimen Kirikae written test? What's the passing score?

It varies by prefecture, but typically 10 true/false questions with a passing standard of 7 correct (70%). Some centres include additional road-sign questions (5 items), requiring a 70%+ total score.

Which countries are exempt from the practical driving test?

Around 29 countries/regions with bilateral agreements with Japan, including Taiwan, Hong Kong, UK, Germany, France, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, South Korea. Drivers from China, the US, India, the Philippines, Vietnam must take the full practical exam. Check your prefectural police website or test centre for the current list.

Can I retake the test if I fail? How long do I wait?

Yes. Retakes can usually be booked the next day, and some test centres allow same-day retakes. Retake fees apply (around ¥2,550). We recommend reviewing your incorrect-answer categories before rebooking.

Can mock tests fully replace the official question bank?

Not 100%, but they cover approximately 80-90% of common question patterns and trick questions. We recommend pairing with official materials from the licence test centre or IsshoHub's Japan Driving Guide.

What are the key tips for true/false questions?

Japanese traffic-rule true/false questions often set traps using absolute phrasings like "in all cases" or "under any condition" — these are usually wrong. Watch for words like "must," "may," "is prohibited," "as a rule" — these often determine the correct answer.

Can I take the quiz without logging in?

How long should I prepare for the practical driving test?

This depends on your driving experience. We generally recommend 2-3 practice runs at a test centre after passing the written exam, to familiarise yourself with Japan-specific course designs (Crank, S-curve, Railway crossing, etc.). Japanese roads are narrow and examiners are strict — allow 1-2 months of preparation.
