Chapter 2 — Road Sign Quick Reference

Road signs are fundamental knowledge for driving in Japan. Use this chapter to quickly look up the meaning of any road sign. Some signs look very similar and are common traps in the driving licence exam — be sure to study Chapter 1 — Easily Confused Signs and Road Markings to master the key differences.
