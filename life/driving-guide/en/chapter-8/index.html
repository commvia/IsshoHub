Chapter 8 — Speed Limits

Speed limits vary by vehicle category, road type, and whether you are towing. This chapter consolidates all speed rules into two tables: standard limits for general roads and expressways, and special limits that apply when towing.

8-1　Speed Limits by Vehicle Category

The limits in the table below are the statutory maximums. If a 最高速度 (Maximum Speed) sign is posted on the road, follow that figure instead — unless the sign shows a number higher than the statutory limit, in which case the lower statutory limit always applies.

🧠 Memory tip

Split the vehicles into two groups — mopeds and everything else:

・Mopeds (原付): maximum 30 km/h on general roads; not allowed on expressways at all.

・All other vehicles: uniform 60 km/h on general roads; expressway minimum is always 50 km/h.

・Expressway maximum: "heavier vehicle = lower ceiling" — 80 < 90 < 100, remember them in that order.

🧠 Towing speed quick-reference

⭐ Motor vehicle towing: "Proper coupling = 60 / Heavy towing light = 40 / Everything else = 30"

　Full equipment = normal speed; heavy vehicle towing light = medium speed; casual breakdown tow = slowest.

⭐ Moped towing: "Towing = 25 / Not towing = 30"

　A moped's normal general-road limit is 30 km/h — add a trailer and it drops to 25.
